Fun Facts About Camels

  • Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes
  • Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat
  • They can drink up to 40 gallons at once
  • They can travel at up to 40 miles per hour


    Fun Facts About Cicadas

    • Cicadas live on all continents except Antarctica
    • They can produce between 80 and 100 decibels
    • Some cicadas could explode with fungus
    • They get their nutrients from tree roots


      Fun Facts About Pigeons

      • Pigeons can recognise each letter of the human alphabet
      • They fly at an average speed of 77.6 mph
      • They can find their way back to their nest from 1300 miles away
      • They are better multitaskers than humans


        Fun Facts About Pandas

        • Pandas learn how to climb at 5 months old
        • They spend 10 - 16 hours eating
        • Newborn pandas are pink
        • Adult panda bears can weigh up to 100 pounds
